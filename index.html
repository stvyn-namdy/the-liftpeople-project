Welcome to LiftPeople

The Lift People project is our response to help curb the spread of COVID'19 and its aftermath impact on the lives of vulnerable Africans. The LiftPeople Project is an intentional commitment to confront these divisions during a global pandemic by convening people from across the globe to donate one dollars and make a difference (funding small organizations and individuals) who are working as a way to curb the spread of COVID'19 and reduce the aftermath economic effect on people.
