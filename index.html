Welcome to The LiftPeople Project

The LiftPeople Project is our response to help curb the spread of COVID'19 and its aftermath impact on the lives of vulnerable Nigerians. It is an intentional commitment to confront issues such as hygiene, health and nutrition during this global pandemic by convening people from across the globe to donate one dollars and make a difference (funding small organizations and individuals) who are working as a way to curb the spread of COVID'19 and reduce the aftermath economic effect on people. Studies have shown that masks fundamentally prevent the transmission from you to others, sneezing, coughing, but also breathing. As a result, we are mobilizing tailors and the fashion communities to produce face masks for individuals unable to purchase or make one themselves. We believe that ever assistance, no matter how small, can go a long a in making a difference in a time like this.
