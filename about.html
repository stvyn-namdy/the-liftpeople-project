About Us

The LiftPeople Project is our response to help curb the spread of COVID'19 and its aftermath impact on the lives of vulnerable Nigerians. It is an intentional commitment to confront issues such as hygiene, health and nutrition during this global pandemic by convening people from across the globe to donate one dollars and make a difference (funding small organizations and individuals) who are working as a way to curb the spread of COVID'19 and reduce the aftermath economic effect on people. Studies have shown that masks fundamentally prevent the transmission from you to others, sneezing, coughing, but also breathing. As a result, we are mobilizing tailors and the fashion communities to produce face masks for individuals unable to purchase or make one themselves. We believe that ever assistance, no matter how small, can go a long a in making a difference in a time like this.

What you aim to achieve

The LiftPeople ProjectLift is a project is the Mustard Seed Foundation response's on Covid-19, augmenting public health services by supporting ordinary people with protective wear (face mask), supplying relevant essential and providing consumables to help reduce the impact of the global pandemic to the most vulnerable in the society. For example, water, food and other basic essentials are being provided to the most vulnerable.

Location of tailors across the State

Our flagship project is a response following the news making rounds on how mask help to curb the spread of COVID'19. We are mobilizing tailors to sew some face masks. As a player in the fashion landscape, this is a challenging time for most startup owners. However, we believe our skill can be redeployed during this global pandemic/crisis. Hence, our decision to organize tailors colleagues to work with us on this initiative to help produce face mask so we can help save lives.

What sort of impact the program would aim to achieve

Solutions: Several areas have been identified as being practical areas where Lift can add value. These are as follows;

  • Reduce Risk of Spread from person to person and community spread.
  • Help reduce the Burden on the State Hospitals and Resources.
  • Supporting primary healthcare workers and Provision of Medical Supplies.
  • Access to Water (bottled water, boreholes & water tankers)
  • Access to Basic Supplies and Provision of Food
